Famous Street Foods of Chennai

  • Idli: Steamed rice cakes often served with chutney and sambar.
  • Dosa: A thin, crispy pancake made from fermented rice and lentil batter, typically served with chutneys.
  • Pongal: A savory dish made from rice and lentils, flavored with spices and often garnished with cashews.
