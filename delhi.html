Famous Street Foods of Delhi

  • Chaat: A savory snack with a mix of flavors, often including potatoes, chickpeas, and yogurt.
  • Paratha: Stuffed flatbreads, often served with pickles and yogurt.
  • Jalebi: A sweet made from deep-fried batter, soaked in sugar syrup.
