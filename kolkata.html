Famous Street Foods of Kolkata

  • Puchka: A popular street snack, similar to Pani Puri, filled with tamarind water and spicy fillings.
  • Kathi Roll: A wrap made with paratha, filled with spiced meat or vegetables.
  • Sandesh: A traditional Bengali sweet made from fresh paneer and flavored with cardamom.
