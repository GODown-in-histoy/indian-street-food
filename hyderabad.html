Famous Street Foods of Hyderabad

  • Biryani: A fragrant rice dish cooked with marinated meat and spices, known for its unique flavors.
  • Haleem: A rich, slow-cooked stew made with meat, lentils, and spices, especially popular during Ramadan.
  • Mirchi Ka Salan: A spicy curry made with green chilies, often served with biryani.
